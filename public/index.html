Our Journey & Vision

Our journey started through highlighting digital information literateness and supportive financial aid among needy school students. Our digital literacy and career mentorship programs organized across the students, youngsters and public over social media campaigns, workshop events and hands-on trainings during our initial stage existences have empowered children, students and public through self-learning and evolution approach, the received ample positive responses and hopes got directed us to further expand the self-enrichment activities amongst the individuals along the direction of advancing their routine life in numerous ways.

Thus, we understood to provide the platform among nurturing talents that would support them to self-progress with literacy and curate the successful small startups in benefiting the community.
